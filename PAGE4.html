THE PSHS-CARAGA REGION CAMPUS (PSHS-CRC) IN BUTUAN CITY IS THE 13TH CAMPUS OF THE PSHS SYSTEM,
CATERING TO GIFTED STUDENTS IN SCIENCE AND MATHEMATICS. SERVING SCHOLARS FROM CARAGA AND
NEIGHBORING PROVINCES, PSHS-CRC HAS BEEN ENRICHING ITS CURRICULUM BASED ON EVIDENCE-BASED
RESEARCH SINCE ITS OPENING IN JUNE 2014. EMBRACING TECHNOLOGY AND DIGITAL TRANSFORMATION,
PSHS-CRC EQUIPS STUDENTS FOR SUCCESS IN THE MODERN WORLD, FOSTERING A LOVE FOR SCIENCE AND
SHAPING THE FUTURE OF YOUNG MINDS.
